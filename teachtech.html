TeachTech2017

Saturday, December 9th

Yale University

Join us for the first ever TeachTech conference, to learn how to integrate coding and computer science in your class!

TeachTech is Code Haven's conference for teachers interested in incorporating computer science curriculum into their classrooms. We will be teaching computer science fundamentals and showing how we've demonstrated concepts to students in a fun and engaging way. We will also be showing software that teachers can implement in their classrooms to make CS more appealing to students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this conference targeted at?

TeachTech is aimed at K-12 teachers with some to no experience teaching computer science. If you have experience and would like to teach a workshop, please let us know!

What do I need to bring?

Please bring a laptop, charger, and questions! Also, if you wish to take notes, please bring your own materials.

What about parking?

Parking lots 16 and 30 are free on weekends. Parking lot 30 is closer to the venue, but may fill up. See this map for further details. There is also metered parking on Hillhouse Avenue.
