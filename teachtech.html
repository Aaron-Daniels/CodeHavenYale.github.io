TeachTech2017

Saturday, December 9th

at Yale University

Join us for the first ever TeachTech conference, to learn how to integrate coding and computer science in your class!

TeachTech is Code Haven's conference for Connecticut teachers interested in incorporating computer science curriculum into their classrooms. We will be teaching computer science fundamentals and showing how we've demonstrated concepts to students in a fun and engaging way. We will also be showing software that teachers can implement in their classrooms to make CS more appealing to students.
