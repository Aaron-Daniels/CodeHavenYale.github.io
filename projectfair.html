Code Haven Project Fair

April 27, 2018

Bishop Woods School

Join us for the first ever Code Haven Project Fair, April 27, 2018 at Bishop Woods Middle School! Take a look at the exciting mobile apps students have made!

Over the past two semesters, we've been using MIT App Inventor to introduce middle schoolers to computer science concepts. The curriculum will end in a final project, which the students will showcase at this project fair.


Schedule

  • 10:30 - 10:40 Attendee Arrival
  • 10:40 - 11:30 Session 1: Bishop Woods and Fair Haven students present
  • 11:30 - 12:20 Lunch sponsored by TSAI City
  • 12:20 - 1:10 Session 2: Saint Martins and Worthington Hooker students present
  • 1:10-1:30 Closing Ceremony and Raffle
We are being generously sponsored and supported by Facebook, Google, Makey Makey, MakeBlock, Ozobot, Piper, littleBits, TSAI City and Yale School of Engineering and Applied Science.
